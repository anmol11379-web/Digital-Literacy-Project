# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist



##  General Safety Tips
-  Never share **OTP, PIN, or passwords** with anyone  
-  Avoid clicking on **unknown or suspicious links**  
-  Always verify calls/messages claiming to be from banks  
-  Use **strong and unique passwords** for accounts  



##  UPI / Online Payment Safety
-  Never approve **unknown payment requests**  
-  Remember: *Receiving money does NOT require a PIN*  
-  Use only **official apps** (Google Pay, PhonePe, etc.)  
-  Always check **receiver details** before sending money
